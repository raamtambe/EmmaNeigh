Kira

Kira is an AI contract intelligence platform that helps legal teams analyze contracts with proven accuracy, flexible governance controls, and workflows designed for high-volume review. Backed by 30 years of legal expertise and refined through a decade of AI innovation, Kira delivers battle-tested precision through a hybrid AI architecture.
